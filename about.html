About Us

Radhe Insurance & Investment is a locally owned and operated independent Insurance and Financial agency. This means that we do not represent just one company, we represent several carriers and institutions. Our agency only utilizes A rated partners, which better enables us to meet the individual needs of our clients. This also allows our clients to stay with us even as their insurance and financial needs evolve or change over time.

What is an Independent Agency?

We are Independent Agents in Springfield, Illinois, free to choose the best carrier for your insurance needs. We do not work for an insurance company; we work for you. We work on your side when you have a loss and follow through to see that you get fair, prompt payment and service. The Group Insurance Agency represents a carefully selected group of financially strong, reputable insurance companies, therefore, we are able to offer you the best coverage at the most competitive price.

With an independent insurance agent, you have choices. Independent agents are not tied to any one insurance company. One of the advantages of using an independent agent is that he or she works to satisfy your needs. You are using an expert for an important financial decision.
